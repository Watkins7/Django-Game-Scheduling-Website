Welcome to Pickup Game!

        This is a community site where you can register a team to play games in real life!

        You can sign in or register by clicking the link below!

        Community rules are:
            1) In order for results to be valid, each team captain must unanimously agree on the decision
            2) Pickup App is not responsible for any injury that may occur through physical meetups

        How this works:
            1) Have your team captain register an account
            2) After logging in to your HOME page, you should see a map displaying teams in the local area
            3) Map markers are clickable to view information about the opposing teams
            4) Use map markers or search bar to navigate to an opponent's calendar
            5) If you find a game your team is interested in attending, book the appointment on the opponent's calendar
            6) If you would like to host a game, navigate to your TEAM page (this link is on your HOME page)
            7) Your TEAM page should function likes settings, you can change your name and host events on your calendar
            8) If you do not see a game that you are interested in, you can create the game!


        How MMR Works:
            1) Every Team starts with 50 points
            2) Losing a match gives you -5 points
            3) Winning a match gives you +5 points
            4) Invalid match results gives 0 points
